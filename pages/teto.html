Kasane Teto

Diva born from a hoax

Origin Story

Created as an April Fool's joke in 2008, Teto started as a fake Vocaloid announcement before becoming a UTAU sensation.

Voice Synthesis

Uses both UTAU and Synthesizer V AI technology. Her voice provider is Japanese voice actress Oyamano Mayo.

Iconic Design

Features pink twin drills, red eyes, and gothic lolita fashion. Her hair drills spin at 900RPM!
